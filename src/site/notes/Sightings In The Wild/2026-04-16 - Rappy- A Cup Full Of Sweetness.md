## A Little Extra Context
This one was done during an art jam with some local creative folk.  One person just happened to have a candlestick in his bag (cuz why not, right?)  

WhenI asked the nice folks behind the counter for an empty cup "for a silly art thing",  she asked if I was the one who left the little cat hanging out in the wooden blinds a while back -- which I was!  Turns out she loved it, and keeps the lil' Zu behind the counter! :D
