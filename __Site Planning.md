## What Is a Whimsy Scoundrel?
The term is twofold:  It's both a *fun little artistic movement*, and a *name for folks who embrace it*!


* Playful little index card critters
* often created on the spot, designed to be interacting with something in the environment
* left with friendly notes on the back, to bring a smile to whoever may find them




## Why?

Life is rough. We're stuck running around this way and that, all caught up in a relentless grind that's often pretty thankless, and it's getting harder and harder to find those little moments in the day that leave us with a sense of genuine joy.

But we don't have to accept this at face value -- **Let's create those moments ourselves!**



## What if... 
* we got to bring a little *whimsy* back into each day? 
* we could push back against these forces of entropy, and reintroduce ourselves to the joys of play?
* we could shift the way we look at the world around us, and remember what it's like to find fun waiting to be explored -- all in simple, everyday places?

Well, we can! 

* **Empowerment**!  We have the ability to make the world feel a little more magic, for one stranger at a time, and everyone they share their joy with as a result. One small bit of whimsy and kindness can ripple out more than we'd ever imagine -- and that's POWERFUL magic right there!
* **Community**!  Being out and about, finding spots to leave little buddies.. Whether you feel like interacting or would rather play quietly, it's a nice way to feel a little more connected to what's going on around us.  All while injecting it with a little extra whimsy and joy. 
* **Creativity**!  How often do we look closely at our surroundings? And I mean really slow things down, and take in the small details?  

Try it now -- look around you, and let your imagination run.  Picture little cartoons interacting with things, scampering about and getting into mischief.  I bet a lot of things could become a lot more interesting, right?
