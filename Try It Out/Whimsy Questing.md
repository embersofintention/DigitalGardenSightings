# Whimsy Questing
---
*If you can doodle, you can Whimsy Quest!*

> [!polaroid|right] Just Hangin' Out
> ![[attachments/2026-04-16-195258--WS.jpg]]

There are a lot of great reasons to go Whimsy Questing.  Here are just a few to get you started: 

* **Empowerment**!  We have the ability to make the world feel a little more magic, for one stranger at a time, and everyone they share their joy with as a result. One small bit of whimsy and kindness can ripple out more than we'd ever imagine -- and that's POWERFUL magic right there!
* **Community**!  Being out and about, finding spots to leave little buddies.. Whether you feel like interacting or would rather play quietly, it's a nice way to feel a little more connected to what's going on around us.  All while injecting it with a little extra whimsy and joy. 
* **Creativity**!  How often do we look closely at our surroundings? And I mean really slow things down, and take in the small details?  Looking around for spots to leave little guys encourages taking on a whole new appreciation for those little things!


Look around you, and let your imagination run.  Picture little cartoons interacting with things, scampering about and getting into mischief.  I bet a lot of things could become a lot more interesting, right?


# Try It Out!
---

All you need to get started is a handful of index cards (or something similar!) to use, and something to draw with.   It's nice to have scissors on hand to cut them out, since it adds that nice little something extra, but really there's no wrong way to do it!

> [!polaroid|left] ready for shenanigans
> ![[Zu-5.png]]

## Building a Whimsy Kit

The Core Essentials: 
* Index cards
* something to write with

The Basics:  
* Scissors - to cut them out, adding to the illusion
* highlighters - trace around the outlines. Makes them really pop!

Excellent Extras: 
* Colored pens - because fun
* white gel pen - add highlights over other details
* straight cuticle scissors - tiny, sharp, great for small details
* self-adhesive googly eyes - instantly makes everything more fun
* tape of any sort - quick fix in case you need to un-cut a cut
* stickers - for adding a little something extra
